Mentorship


Providing transitioning military, veterans and their families peer-to-peer software mentorship, coaching and guidance for gainful employment as software developers.



Hardware/Software Requirements:

  • Modern Computer
  • Internet Connection
  • Browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari will do)
  • Terminal (iTerm2, stock)
  • An IDE or Text Editor (Eclipse, Atom, Brackets, etc.)

Scholarships


We help bridge the gap between the VA and Code Schools and Code Conferences. We partner with tech conferences around the country and offer scholarship tickets to events throughout the year.

Our scholarships to code schools provide opportunities for the military community to kickstart their careers in software development.
